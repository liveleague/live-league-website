About


What is Live League?

Live League is the UK's only ongoing 'battle of the bands' style competition.


Entry is open to artists of any genre and events will be hosted by promoters across the country.


Points are allocated to artists when you vote and the number of points allocated is equal to the ticket price. For example, a Â£5 ticket gives you a vote worth 5 points.



What are the prizes?

Every time a vote is cast, money is added to the prize pool.


As the league progresses, sponsorship will play a major role in boosting the prize pool.



When does it finish?

The first season of the league will last from September 2019 to April 2020.


Then, in the summer of 2020, we'll round off the season with a grand finale . More details to follow.
